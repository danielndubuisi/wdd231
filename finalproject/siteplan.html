Project Site Plan

Site Name: SoundSphere

The name “SoundSphere” evokes a global, immersive, and community-centered experience around music. It implies a space where users can discover, share, and engage with music in all its forms.

Logo

Optional domain: soundspherehub.com

Site Purpose

The site serves as a music discovery and community hub where users can explore new artists, listen to curated playlists, share their own music tastes, and connect with others. It highlights indie musicians, music news, and allows fans to interact and discuss everything music-related.

User Scenarios

  • How can I discover new indie music similar to my favorite artists?
  • Can I create and share a playlist with my friends through this site?
  • Does this site provide information on the history and roots of my favorite music genres?

Color Schema

  • Primary color: Neon Green (#00ff7f) - Used for headings and navigations
  • Secondary color: Electric Pink (#ff1493) - Used for call-to-action buttons, icons, and hover effects.
  • Accent color: Subtle Green Gray (#2a302f) - Used for backgrounds, cards, and some sections to create contrast and improve readability.
  • Dark background: Deep Charcoal (#1a1a1a) - Used for background to create a modern feel that is easy on the eye.

Typography

  • Primary Font: Montserrat – Used for body text. Clean, legible and modern feel.
  • Secondary Font: Lato – Used for headings, bold text and navigation for an inviting and friendly look.
